FAQs on IIT JEE Mathematics Important Topics for JEE Exams

▼ 1. What are the essential Maths topics to focus on for IIT JEE preparation?

Key topics include Coordinate Geometry, Calculus, 3D Geometry, Algebra, and Trigonometry, crucial for success in both JEE Main and Advanced.

▼ 2. How can I prioritize my preparation for JEE Main Maths?

Focus on high-weightage topics like Coordinate Geometry, Calculus, 3D Geometry, and Algebra. Analyze previous papers for a better understanding.

▼ 3. Are there additional topics to consider for JEE Advanced Maths preparation?

Yes, delve deeper into Conic Section, Circle, Vector, Trigonometric Equations, and Probability for a comprehensive understanding of JEE Advanced.

▼ 4. Which books are recommended for Maths preparation for IIT JEE?

Refer to reliable study materials like RD Sharma, S.L. Loney, and M.L. Khanna for a thorough understanding of the essential Maths topics.

▼ 5. How should I approach Maths differently for JEE Main and Advanced?

Recognize that JEE Advanced requires a more in-depth understanding of topics. Focus on logic, practice regular mock tests, and understand the difference in approach between Main and Advanced.

▼ 5. Is Ncert enough for JEE Mains Maths ?

Many JEE Main toppers and exam experts suggest students study Maths from NCERT books for JEE Main. NCERT books can help students strengthen their concepts for JEE Main but we recommend students to practice questions from other books as well so that they can learn how to solve advanced-level questions.

Important Tips on JEE Main 2022 Mathematics Preparation

Now that we have understood what kind of questions can be expected in the JEE Main Maths section and what is the general difficulty level of JEE Main 2022 Session 1 examination, we can create a study strategy for the next year or even for the next JEE Main Session. Given below are some of the best tips from our JEE Main Maths experts.

  1. Focus more on Calculus and Algebra as many questions are asked from these two topics.
  2. Make sure to get your doubts solved as soon as possible if you are preparing for JEE Main 2022 Session 2 exam in July.
  3. Create a formula sheet for JEE Main 2022. Include all the important formulae for every topic you have studied. If you know any short-cut tricks, mention them on your formula sheet. Read this sheet at least once a day and revise all the formulae.
  4. Make your conceptual knowledge strong. Focus on understanding how to apply the concept rather than just learning how to use the formula. Once you know how to apply the concept, it will be much easier to solve the length of questions in the exam.
  5. Solve past year papers and sample papers every day. Practicing a variety of questions will help you prepare for all kinds of questions that can be asked in JEE Main. It will also make you familiar with the exam pattern of JEE Main.
  6. Keep revising the topics as it will make it easier to remember the concepts. Pay special attention to topics frequently asked in the examination.
  7. Get the right books and study materials that can help you solve questions and practice as much as possible. Recommended books include:
    • R.D. Sharma for Class XI & XII
    • Trigonometry by S.L. Loney
    • Coordinate Geometry by S.L. Loney
    • Higher Algebra by Hall Knight
    • Problems in Calculus of One Variable by L.A. Maron
